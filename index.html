About Ryan

Ryan is a data analytics enthusiast. He is an Indonesian currently living in the Netherlands, working as a Data Engineer at Van Oord. Ryan finished his study MSc Hydraulic Engineering from Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands, and BSc Ocean Engineering from Bandung Institute of Techonology, Indonesia. Even though he has a really strong background in civil engineering, he is really enthusiast in data analytics, while also digitalizing the engineering process to ease and accelerate the work, thus making it more robust and efficient. This is why he chose to do his thesis in developing an automated tool for container terminal design using Python.

He is interested to work with people/organizations that want to make effective use of their data, starting from data collection, data processing, and data visualizations. This website is made during his free time as a showcase of the projects that he is doing (or have done) for his data analytics skill development.
